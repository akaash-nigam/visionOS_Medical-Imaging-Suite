# Medical Imaging Suite

> Life-size medical scan visualization and surgical planning for Apple Vision Pro

## Overview

Medical Imaging Suite transforms medical imaging workflows by enabling physicians to view patient scans at life-size scale, compare multiple imaging modalities side-by-side in space, draw surgical plans directly on 3D anatomical models, and collaborate with specialists globally in shared spatial environments.

## Key Features

- **Life-Size Scan Visualization** - CT, MRI, X-ray scans at actual anatomical scale
- **Side-by-Side Comparison** - Display multiple scans simultaneously in 3D space
- **Surgical Planning Tools** - Draw, measure, and annotate on 3D models
- **Global Collaboration** - Multi-user spatial sessions with specialists worldwide
- **PACS Integration** - Seamless integration with hospital systems
- **AI-Assisted Analysis** - Automated detection, segmentation, and quantification
